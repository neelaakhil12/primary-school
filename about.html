About Our School

Nurturing young minds and building character through quality primary education.

Our School Overview

Disney Oaks Primary School is a dedicated institution for Grade 1 to Grade 5 students. We provide a stimulating and supportive environment where children can thrive academically, socially, and emotionally.

Our curriculum is designed to spark curiosity and foster a lifelong love for learning. With state-of-the-art facilities and a team of passionate educators, we ensure every child receives the attention and guidance they need to succeed.

Our Mission

To provide a holistic and inclusive primary education that empowers students to reach their full potential, fostering academic excellence, social responsibility, and creative thinking.

Our Vision

To be a leading primary school recognized for its nurturing environment, innovative teaching practices, and commitment to developing well-rounded, compassionate leaders of the future.
