Academic Programs

A comprehensive and engaging curriculum designed for Grades 1 to 5.

Curriculum Overview

Our primary curriculum is designed to balance academic rigor with creative exploration. We follow a child-centric approach that encourages questioning, critical thinking, and collaborative learning. Our subjects include Language Arts, Mathematics, Environmental Studies, Science, Social Studies, and ICT.

Grade 1 & 2

The Foundation Years

Focus on literacy, numeracy, and basic social skills. We use activity-based learning to make transitions smooth and enjoyable.

  • Phonics & Language Skills
  • Basic Math Concepts
  • Environmental Awareness
Grade 3

Skill Strengthening

Deepening understanding of core subjects and introducing more complex problem-solving techniques.

  • Reading Comprehension
  • Mathematical Reasoning
  • Creative Writing
Grade 4

Advanced Understanding

Encouraging independent research and project-based learning. Building a strong base for scientific and logical inquiry.

  • Inquiry-based Science
  • Analytical Math
  • Digital Literacy
Grade 5

Leadership & Readiness

Preparing students for the transition to middle school with a focus on leadership, time management, and complex academic themes.

  • Leadership Programs
  • Advanced Science & Tech
  • Public Speaking

Beyond the Classroom

Holistic development through a variety of co-curricular activities.

Arts & Crafts

Developing creativity and fine motor skills through various artistic mediums.

Music & Dance

Expressing rhythm and movement while building confidence and discipline.

Sports & PE

Promoting physical health, teamwork, and fair play through various games.

Computer Science

Introduction to basic coding, digital safety, and productivity tools.

Dedicated to providing a nurturing and excellence-driven educational environment.
