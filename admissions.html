Admissions 2026-27

Join the The Basics family. We follow a transparent and parent-friendly admission process.

Our Admission Process

Simplified 4-step process for a smooth enrollment journey.

1

Application

Submit the online enquiry form or visit the school campus to collect the application form.

2

Interaction

Parents and students are invited for an informal interaction with our team.

3

Verification

Submission of required documents for verification and review.

4

Confirmation

Payment of fees to secure the seat and complete the enrollment process.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Grade 1: Child must be 6+ years as of 31st March.
  • Grade 2-5: Based on previous school performance.
  • Admission subject to seat availability.

Documents Required

  • Birth Certificate (Original & Copy)
  • Previous Year Report Card
  • Transfer Certificate (TC)
  • 4 Passport Size Photographs
  • Address Proof (Aadhar/Voter ID)
